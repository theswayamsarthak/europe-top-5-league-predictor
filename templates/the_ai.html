THE ARCHITECTURE

Football is often called the beautiful game, driven by emotion, chaos, and moments of individual brilliance.

We believe that within that chaos lies a signal. kickoff.ai is not about gut feelings or punditry. It is a purely mathematical attempt to quantify the future using historical data, market inefficiencies, and statistical modeling.

: : THE TRINITY

The Consensus Engine. Trinity is an ensemble classifier that aggregates votes from three distinct algorithms: XGBoost (Gradient Boosting), Random Forest, and Logistic Regression.
